National Crime Records Bureau

All data was collected from NCRB's official website. NCRB was set-up in 1986 to function as a repository of information on crime and criminals so as to assist the investigators in linking crime to the perpetrators. NCRB brings out three annual reports i.e. Crime in India, Accidental Deaths & Suicides in India and Prison Statistics India. These reports are principal reference points for police officers, researchers, media& policy makers. This map draws data from the Crime in India Reports for the Year 2018, 2017 and 2016.

Crime Against Women Statistics

This map was created to provide easy access to data to local citizens of India, especially women about the changing levels of safety in different states over the past 3 years. Interactive maps can not only help local governments to observe safety trends but also tourists around the world in making better, more-informed decision about their travel to India. The map extracts information on Rank of Criminality by crime rate in different states from the crime reports produced by NCRB and categorise the risk level into five broad categories.
